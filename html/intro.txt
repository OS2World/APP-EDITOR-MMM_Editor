MMM is as small multi-platform text editor
with some hypertext features.
It supports regular expressions search (as GREP util. in UNIX),
fully tunable keybioard & codepage layout,
macrocommands (sequences of keystrokes & programming language).

Platforms supported: i386 PC
    + Linux console
    + IBM OS/2 Warp+ (maybe 2.* +)
    + MS Windows 95/NT console
    + MS DOS (& clones)
